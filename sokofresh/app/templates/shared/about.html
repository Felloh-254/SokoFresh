About SokoFresh

Connecting Communities Through Fresh, Local Produce

Our Story

SokoFresh was born from a simple yet powerful vision: to bridge the gap between local farmers and communities seeking fresh, quality produce. Founded in the heart of Kenya, we recognized that while farmers were growing exceptional fruits and vegetables, consumers often struggled to access truly fresh, locally-sourced produce.

Our name "SokoFresh" combines the Swahili word "Soko" (meaning market) with "Fresh," representing our commitment to creating a vibrant marketplace where freshness meets convenience. We believe that everyone deserves access to nutritious, farm-fresh produce while supporting the hardworking farmers who make it all possible.

🎯 Our Mission

To revolutionize the fresh produce supply chain by creating a seamless digital marketplace that connects local farmers directly with consumers, ensuring fair prices for farmers and fresh, quality produce for families.

🌟 Our Vision

To become the leading platform for farm-to-table commerce in East Africa, fostering sustainable agriculture and healthy communities through technology and innovation.
